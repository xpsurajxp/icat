Who We Are

Computer Education for Students, Beginners and Job Seekers

iCat Compuer Academy provides Basic Courses, Career Courses, Skill Training and Job Oriented computer education for students, beginners and job seekers.

We focus on practical learning so every student can confidently use digital tools in academics, business, office work and professional life.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make quality computer education accessible and meaningful by blending foundational knowledge with job-ready practical skills.

  • Skill-based practice and project work
  • Career guidance with affordable programs
